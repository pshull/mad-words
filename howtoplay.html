How to Play MadWords

Suitable for a group of 2 to 5 people

How to Play

  1. Pick a story template from the main page.
  2. Fill in the blanks with the requested words.
  3. Click "Generate Story" to see your completed MadWord.
  4. Read the story in your group and enjoy the laughs!

Tips for Maximum Fun

  • Play in a group (!!!) and take turns filling in the blanks.
  • Use silly or unexpected words for the funniest results.
  • Try different themes to keep things fresh and exciting.
